About SkinSaver

Your ultimate skincare product price comparison platform.

Our Mission

SkinSaver was created as part of a 24-hour hackathon to solve a common problem: finding the best deals on skincare products across multiple e-commerce platforms. With so many online stores selling the same products at different prices, it can be time-consuming to manually check each site for the best deal.

Our mission is to simplify this process by automatically collecting and comparing prices from popular e-commerce sites like Amazon, Flipkart, and Nykaa, helping you save both time and money.

How It Works

SkinSaver uses advanced web scraping technology to collect real-time price information from multiple e-commerce platforms. Our system:

  • Regularly crawls popular skincare product listings from major retailers
  • Normalizes and compares the data to find the best deals
  • Provides you with up-to-date price comparisons
  • Links directly to the original product listings for easy purchasing

Features

Search & Compare

Search for specific skincare products and instantly see price comparisons across multiple platforms.

Price Tracking

Our system regularly updates prices to ensure you're getting the most current information.

Best Deals

We highlight the best deals to make your decision-making process easier.

Direct Links

One-click access to the original product listings for seamless purchasing.

Our Tech Stack

SkinSaver was built using the following technologies:

  • Backend: Flask (Python web framework)
  • Web Scraping: Beautiful Soup, Requests, Trafilatura
  • Scheduling: APScheduler for periodic data updates
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript

The Team

SkinSaver was developed by a dedicated team of developers during a 24-hour hackathon. Our team combined expertise in web development, data scraping, and UI/UX design to create this platform in a limited timeframe.
